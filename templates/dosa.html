Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cup Idli rice
  • 1/2 tablespoon Fenugreek seeds
  • 5 tablespoon Sesame oil
  • 1 1/2 cup Urad dal
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • Water as required

Preparation Method

  1. Soak the rice and dal separately for 3 hours
  2. Grind dal first into a smooth consistency adding enough water
  3. Grind rice into a smooth batter by adding just enough water to get it going
  4. Combine the two, add salt, and let it ferment in a large enough bowl lightly covered, for about 6-8 hours in a warm place
  5. Add enough water to the fermented batter until you have a smooth, pouring consistency. Make sure the batter is not too watery though, it should be thick-ish but fall off your spoon easily.
  6. Heat a tawa or iron griddle and grease with the oil lightly. Pour about Â½ cup batter to the centre of the tawa.
  7. Using the back of your ladle, gently spread the batter with a circular motion from the centre towards the sides of the tawa.
  8. Drizzle about 1 tsp of oil around the edges of the dosa and add a few sprinkles on the top as well.
  9. If your tawa is greased adequately and is not sticky, the edge of the dosa will start to come off the pan in about 1 minute or so. Use a spatula to gently lift the dosa and flip it over. Cook the other side for another 40 seconds or so and remove from pan.
  10. Serve with dosa podi, chutney or sambar.
